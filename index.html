Epic Games of Glory

Peckish Playoffs

Classic Survival Games with a twist! Navigate our expansive Japanese-themed arena where teams spawn scattered among rivals. Reunite before grace period ends, survive the closing border, and fight with balanced loot that ensures everyone gets a fair chance at victory!

Block Shuffle

Quick reflexes meet pattern recognition! Stand on glazed terracotta blocks with unique shapes and patterns. When a pattern is called, find it fast or fall into the void. One wrong step and you're eliminated in this intense survival challenge!

King of the Hill

Four teams, two arenas, one hilltop to rule! Race to capture and hold the high ground in this classic territory control battle. Arena winners face off in an epic finale - first team to 200 points claims the crown!

Parkour Run

Navigate winding pathways floating over the endless void at sunset! Earn points at themed checkpoints and bonus points for completing the full course within 10 minutes. Remember: when in doubt, just JUMP!

Hide and Seek

Sneak through our sus "Skold" space station! Hide behind pipes, zip through vents, and avoid the self-destruct button. Shrunken hiders must evade team seekers in this starry sci-fi thriller. Survival means points!

SMP Triathlon

Race through our fantasy European mountain castle! Navigate jump pads, levitation zones, and avoid potholes while admiring waterfalls and duels. Placement-based scoring rewards finding the fastest path down the mountain!

The Gauntlet

The ultimate finale! Top two teams battle head-to-head in an epic 1v1 showdown. But beware - chaotic disasters will strike mid-battle, turning strategy into survival. Only one team can emerge victorious from this final test!
