Peckish Playoffs

Classic Survival Games meets Japanese elegance! Navigate our expansive Japanese-themed arena where teams spawn scattered among rivals. The balanced loot system ensures everyone gets a fair chance at victory while the closing border and environmental dangers keep the pressure mounting.

  • Japanese-themed arena with stunning architecture
  • Scattered team spawns for strategic reunification
  • Balanced loot pool for fair gameplay
  • Closing border creates intense final moments
  • Individual scoring for kills, survival, and victory
🎯

Block Shuffle

Test your reflexes and pattern recognition skills! Stand on glazed terracotta blocks featuring unique patterns like circles, squares, and triangles. When a pattern is announced, find it fast or face elimination as wrong blocks disappear beneath your feet.

  • Unique glazed terracotta pattern recognition
  • Fast-paced elimination gameplay
  • Multiple pattern types to master
  • Instant elimination for wrong choices
  • Last player standing wins
👑

King of the Hill

Classic territory control at its finest! Four teams battle across two epic arenas to capture and hold the high ground. Race to the hilltop, secure the capture point, and defend your position. Winners from each arena clash in the ultimate finale.

  • Dual arena setup for maximum action
  • Capture point mechanics
  • First to 200 points claims victory
  • Arena winners face off in finale
  • Strategic positioning is key
🏃‍♂️

Parkour Run

Navigate breathtaking pathways floating over the endless void at sunset! Each themed obstacle presents unique challenges while checkpoints reward your progress. Complete the entire course within 10 minutes for massive bonus points!

  • Stunning sunset void setting
  • Themed obstacle sections
  • Checkpoint-based scoring system
  • 10-minute time limit challenge
  • Bonus points for course completion
👁️

Hide and Seek

Infiltrate our mysterious "Skold" space station! Sneak behind pipes and cables, zip through the vent system, and avoid detection. Hiders are shrunk down to fit in small spaces while seekers hunt relentlessly. Just don't press that self-destruct button!

  • Sci-fi space station setting
  • Shrinking mechanic for tight spaces
  • Interactive vent system
  • Environmental hazards to avoid
  • Time-based survival scoring
🏰

SMP Triathlon

Race through our fantasy European mountain castle! Navigate jump pads, levitation zones, and treacherous potholes while avoiding distracting waterfalls and epic duels. Find the fastest route down the mountain in this placement-based challenge.

  • Fantasy European castle theme
  • Jump pads and levitation mechanics
  • Environmental obstacles and distractions
  • Multiple route options
  • Placement-based scoring system
⚡

The Gauntlet

The ultimate finale showdown! The top two teams face off in an epic 1v1 battle, but chaos reigns supreme as random disasters strike mid-combat. Strategy meets survival in this unpredictable final test that will crown the ultimate SMP champions.

  • Epic finale 1v1 format
  • Random disaster events
  • Chaotic but strategic gameplay
  • Winner-takes-all championship
  • Most intense challenge of all
